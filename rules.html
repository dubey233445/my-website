Rules & Regulations

  1. Designs must be original and created entirely by the team.
  2. Safety guidelines must be strictly followed, and violations may lead to disqualification.
  3. Misconduct, cheating, or tampering is prohibited. Participants must respect others and avoid disrupting the event or harassing organizers and judges.
  4. Damage to property during the event will result in penalties, including disqualification and fines.
  5. In case of ambiguity, the organizers' decision will be final and binding, and no further appeals will be entertained.
  6. Judges' decisions are final. Participants must align their work with the judging criteria.
  7. Participants are encouraged to bring extension cords or power strips for their devices to avoid delays.
  8. This is a competition, not a presentation. Your robots must actively compete with other robots.

Special Note for Drone Racing:

The drone race will allow mini-drones only. The drone that completes the obstacle course in the shortest time will be declared the winner. This same timing-based competition structure applies to all robot challenges.
